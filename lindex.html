Guindy National Park

Click here to view the map.

Information:

The #2 hotspot of Chennai, it houses around 200 birds(including migrants). Some highlights include:

  • Malayan Night heron
  • Oriental Turtle Dove
  • Dollarbird
  • Orange-breasted Green Pigeon
  • Eurasian Hobby
    • It is full of a great variety of flora and fauna. For animals, some endangered species include the blackbuck, and various snake species, a type of pit viper being the latest. It is a protected area and you will have to have permission to enter. The Guindy Children's park is in the adjacent compound. This place doesn't face many threats since it is protected much more than Pallikaranai, except for occasional poaching and logging.
