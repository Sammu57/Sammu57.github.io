STOP FEEDING PIGEONS and SAVE LIVES!

5/14/2021

0 Comments

 
Watch this video, it is very helpful. I was actually coughing too much for a few days. That's when my mother remembered one point that we learnt from Mr. Owl's Classroom by Iragukal Amritha Nature Trust. It was that, "Don't stay with pigeons too long. It could affect your lungs." Today, I went to my terrace, kept food, changed the water, and saw many pigeons as well as parakeets coming to eat. Then I came down and remembered that I wanted to check if there was any video on this problem, and there was this one. 

                                 You see, pigeons are getting abundant food nowadays, and they are enjoying it. You can see many people feeding them on the roads, streets, and other places. This has to be stopped. I too did the same mistake. Since I've seen this now, I will stop feeding them and only provide water. 

                                                                                                               But the thing is that I am a bit sad along with being happy of getting more knowledge for the reason that I won't be able to see my avian friends coming to eat any more... Any way, lessons learnt!
