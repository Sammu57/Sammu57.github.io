Information:

The Theosophical Society, located near the Adayaru Estuary, is the #5 bird hotspot of Chennai, with more than 170 species(including migrants). Some highlights are:

  • Forest Wagtail
  • Brown-rumped Minivet
  • Gray-fronted Green Pigeon
  • Greater Racket-tailed Drongo
  • Black-capped Kingfisher

    • Since it is near the Adayaru Estuary, the Theosophical Society shares a few species like the Gray-fronted Green Pigeon. One might need permission to enter. Also, there are many trees inside, making it an ideal habitat for many birds.
