Information:

The #4 hotspot of Chennai, the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras houses more than 190 species of birds(including migrants). Some highlights are:

  • Eastern Yellow Wagtail
  • Brown Boobook
  • Lesser Frigatebird
  • Lesser Cuckoo
  • Ruddy-breasted Crake
    • With a huge campus covered with nature, IIT Madras has got a lot of wildlife inside. Blackbucks, Spotted deer or Chital, snakes such as Rat Sankes and a huge variety of birds, butterflies and other insects make up excellent biodiversity. It is a campus full of life.
